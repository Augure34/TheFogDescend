description=[h1]The Fog Descend (Build 42)[/h1]
description=
description=A thick, terrifying toxic fog rolls across Kentucky... and they are coming.
description=
description=Inspired by the classic mod "The Darkness Is Coming," [b]The Fog Descend[/b] introduces a periodic world event that transforms Project Zomboid into a tense fight for survival.
description=
description=[h2]☣️ Toxic Fog & Gas Mask Protection[/h2]
description=The fog is no longer just a visual hazard; it is a deadly poison.
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Toxicity Accumulation:[/b] Being outside in the fog builds up toxicity, causing severe fatigue, pain, loss of endurance, and eventually death.
description=[*] [b]Indoor Safety:[/b] Retreating indoors shields you from the toxic air and allows your body to slowly recover.
description=[*] [b]Gas Mask Protection:[/b] Wearing a functional gas mask (vanilla or modded) blocks toxicity build-up completely.
description=[*] [b]Build 42 Compatibility:[/b] Automatically recognizes vanilla gas masks, custom-registered modded masks, and any items carrying the B42 [code]gasmask[/code] tag!
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]⚙️ Event Presets[/h2]
description=Every setting is active across all presets by default (including warning sirens, radio alerts, sprinter zombie mutations, and vehicle malfunctions). Only the timing and intensity sliders are adjusted:
description=
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Normal Preset (Balanced):[/b] The default experience. The fog descends roughly every week (5-10 days) and lasts from 1 to 3 days. Toxicity takes 12 hours of constant exposure to kill.
description=[*] [b]Hardcore Preset (Chaotic & Hostile):[/b] Drastically reduces safety guardrails. Fog can trigger as early as 2 days in, last up to 5 days, and toxicity will kill you in just 6 hours. Cooldowns and duration are highly random.
description=[*] [b]Custom Preset:[/b] Fine-tune all settings (durations, cooldowns, trigger chances, death times) to create your own unique setup.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]🌟 Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Dynamic Weather Event:[/b] Thick, desaturated fog rolls in, casting the world into an eerie gloom.
description=[*] [b]Emergency Broadcasts:[/b] The Automated Emergency Broadcast System (AEBS) radio channel will issue warning alerts before the fog rolls in.
description=[*] [b]Warning Sirens:[/b] Ominous air raid sirens sound when the fog begins.
description=[*] [b]Zombie Mutation:[/b] Zombies morph into fast, aggressive sprinters with hyper-alert sight, hearing, and intelligence during the fog.
description=[*] [b]Vehicle Malfunctions:[/b] The electromagnetic fog risks stalling your car engines, potentially leaving you stranded.
description=[/list]
